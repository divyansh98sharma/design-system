## How to navigate

Use the **sidebar** on the left to browse sections:

- **Design System** — Introduction (here), Philosophy, Getting Started, Accessibility, Contributing, and Changelog.
- **Foundations** — Token-level colour palette, typography scale, spacing, and icon reference.
- **Atoms** — Indivisible UI elements: Button, Input Field, Chip, Toggle, Tooltip, and more.
- **Molecules** — Composed groups of atoms: Disclaimer, Toast, Popover, Breadcrumb, and more.
- **Organisms** — Complex, self-contained sections: Tabs, Table, Modal, Top Nav, Side Navigation.
- **Templates** — Page-level skeletons assembled from organisms *(roadmap)*.
- **Pages** — Templates filled with representative real-world content *(roadmap)*.
