## Atomic design context

In the atomic design hierarchy, **Templates** sit above Organisms:

| Layer | Role |
|-------|------|
| Atoms | Indivisible elements (Button, Input, Toggle) |
| Molecules | Groups of atoms (Disclaimer, Toast, Popover) |
| Organisms | Complex sections (Table, Modal, Navigation) |
| **Templates** | **Page skeletons assembled from organisms** |
| Pages | Templates filled with real content |
