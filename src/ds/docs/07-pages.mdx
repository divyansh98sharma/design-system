## The difference between Templates and Pages

<div className="pg-callout">
  <strong>Templates</strong> define the layout and component composition with placeholder
  content — lorem ipsum, avatar initials, skeleton shapes.
  <br /><br />
  <strong>Pages</strong> are instances of templates with real content: actual patient names,
  realistic encounter data, genuine form fields — making design decisions concrete and testable.
</div>

## Why document pages in Storybook?

| Use case | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| Stakeholder reviews | Share a Chromatic URL instead of a live environment |
| Usability testing | Seed stories with task-specific data sets |
| Regression testing | Catch layout shifts when tokens or organisms change |
| Onboarding | New engineers see the full system assembled, not just atoms |
